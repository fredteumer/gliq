# Battle Royale Legends — Game Design Document

**Studio:** Apex Vision Interactive
**Requested funding:** $4,200,000
**Target release:** Q4 2027

## Concept

*Battle Royale Legends* is a free-to-play 100-player battle royale shooter with
a modern military setting. Players drop onto a shrinking map, scavenge weapons,
and fight to be the last squad standing.

## Core mechanics

- 100-player last-squad-standing matches
- Shrinking play zone on a timer
- Weapon and armor scavenging with rarity tiers
- Seasonal battle pass with cosmetic unlocks

## Art & audio

Photorealistic modern military aesthetic. Licensed soundtrack.

## Platforms & pricing

PC and consoles. Free to play, monetized through a battle pass and cosmetic
storefront.

## Team

Twelve people. First commercial title as a studio.
