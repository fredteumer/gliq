# Hollow Reef — Game Design Document

**Studio:** Tidewrack Games
**Requested funding:** $850,000
**Target release:** Q3 2027

## Concept

*Hollow Reef* is a hand-drawn 2D metroidvania set in a drowned, bioluminescent
city. The player is a salvage diver who gains traversal abilities by grafting
salvaged machinery onto their diving rig, progressively unlocking a dense,
interconnected map.

## Core mechanics

- Ability-gated exploration across a single continuous map
- Oxygen management as a soft timer, creating tension in unexplored areas
- Grafting system: each recovered artifact grants both a traversal ability and a combat option
- Boss encounters that permanently reshape regions of the map

## Art & audio

Hand-drawn 2D with a limited bioluminescent palette against near-black
backgrounds. Original orchestral-ambient score.

## Platforms & pricing

Windows and Nintendo Switch at launch, PlayStation post-launch. Intended price
$19.99, no microtransactions, one paid expansion planned.

## Team

Seven people. The art director and lead designer previously shipped two
commercially successful 2D action titles.
