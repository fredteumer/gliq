# ROANOAKE

## Concept
ROANOAKE is based on the events of the Roanoake colony () in North Carolina. 

## Core mechanics
- A linear, cRPG
- 16th century english colonists in unexplored American southeast swamp/wilderness
- first person (morrowind-style) 
- collect a party from the ship and go exploring (autumn landing)
  - chapter 0: ship interior, collect party. when on ship deck, see something cosmic, shipwreck
  - chapter 1: simple fetch quests with minor combat to establish a camp
  - chapter 2: camp is established, fight indians, establish the colony and help with some simple quests, broken with pirate+indian invasion, but repelled
  - chapter 3: first snowfall after the colony is established, young women start going missing, venture out after one of them with your party. start to fight with the touched and finish with cosmic entities
  - chapter 4: colony in shambles, the mayor is losing his mind. unravel his madness by understanding he has a tunnel system to hidden ritual with the witch coven. rising action
  - chapter 5: after heading to stop the ritual, engage in a final fight with the colonial mayor's coven, then the rising entity. stop the ritual but in the process destroy the colony and all is lost. 

An approximately 10 hours experience from start to finish. Difficult, with some resident evil survival horror meets cRPG-style mechanics. Turn based combat on a grid with 3D first-person exploration.

## Art & audio
PS1 3D graphics with chunky pixels and textures. Dripping with atmosphere of mild horror. Sound design inspired by the film *The VVitch* (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Witch_(2015_film))
