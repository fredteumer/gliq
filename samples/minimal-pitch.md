# Tidal Rift

## Concept

Tidal Rift is a hand-drawn 2D metroidvania set in a flooded, bioluminescent
city. The player is a salvage diver who explores a single interconnected map,
unlocking traversal abilities from recovered gear.

## Core mechanics

- Ability-gated exploration across one continuous map
- Oxygen management as a soft timer in unexplored areas
- Boss encounters that permanently reshape regions of the map

## Art & audio

Hand-drawn 2D with a limited colour palette. Atmospheric original score.

## Platforms & pricing

Windows and macOS at launch. Priced at $14.99, with no microtransactions.
